Work Process

What can I say, I'm a creature of habit. I love getting things done.

I get an idea, make a gameplan, and execute. I psuedo-code everything. For each project, I have sheets of blank paper filled with ideas. Those ideas are then rapidly turned into code that is likely broken. Broken code is then fixed into nice, readable working code. Did I also say I love bug-fixing?

1. Idea
2. Sketch
3. Design
4. Develop
5. Test
6. Deploy

And back again. There is always a time and place for Waterfall development. I've embraced the Agile and Extreme Programming (XP) methodologies and have seen first hand the usefullness of them. I like to code iteratively and get the product into the customers hands in weeks instead of years.
